GAME DEVELOPMENT SHOWCASE

Ever since I booted up Roblox for the first time back in third grade, I've wanted to make games. Now, I do.

I program mainly with C# in the Unity engine, create and animate sprites with Aseprite, and compose music with the Reaper DAW and various plugins. This is just a showcase of my best works, you can find every game I've built on my Itch.io page.

Game development is especially challenging as a solo developer - AKA the game designer, programmer, art director, music producer, and marketer, all at once. Though I'm making progress, I am nowhere near the height of my potential, and I fully intend to build many more games for years to come.

MARIANNE

DON'T GET TOO LOST DOWN THERE

A short exploration and light horror game where, a mother whale, you must explore the caves and deep waters to find your missing calf. For this game I took a more poetic, artistic perspective on game development, focusing on atmosphere and symbolism. into game development to tell a story about diving into the depths. Built within 7 days for Brackeys Game Jam 2023.2.

BURNUP!

ONE BUTTON ASTEROID DEFENSE

A quick and intense astroid defense game designed to only need the spacebar to play. Built within 48 hours for Mini Jam 162: Heat, with the creative limitation of only one button performing two actions. Ranked top 5 out of 139 total entries. This was my first 48-hour game jam so I didn't expect the high placement, but a win is a win.

SPITFIRE

A BIPLANE SHOOTER FRENZY

A classic shoot-em-up action game with dodge rolls, unique enemies, and bossfights. Built within 14 days under the creative limitation of a 64x64 resolution screen. This was my first go at a true independent creation without needing to follow a tutorial, and I think it went very well as far as first times go.
